Designed for tiny photographers

Lemon Squeezy keeps things simple at first, then unlocks more as your kid grows — so they can learn camera skills without getting lost in settings.

One huge shutter button

The camera screen is stripped down to the essentials. The shutter button is big, bold, and easy to hit — and you can go even further with “Clumsy Fingers” mode, which makes (almost) the entire screen a tap target.

Photos stay in the app, not the camera roll

Apple’s Camera app is a sneaky back door into your entire photo library. Lemon Squeezy keeps kids’ snaps separate in a safe in-app gallery, so they can review their photos without scrolling through yours.

Daily limits and cooldown timers

Set a daily photo limit and an optional cooldown after each shot. It’s a gentle way to slow things down, prevent rapid-fire “click click click,” and keep storage under control.

Nice and simple:

When the daily limit is reached, playtime’s over.

Settings that adapt to your kid as they grow

Start with the basics, then gradually introduce extra controls as they get more confident — like tap to focus and pinch to zoom. You decide what’s available, and when.

Widgets, quick access, themes, and quality settings*

Want to go beyond the basics? Pro members can customize the camera’s look, add widgets, launch from the Lock Screen, and choose higher (or lower) quality photos depending on your storage needs.

* Available to Pro members.

FAQ

Does this replace the iPhone Camera app?

It can — but it’s mainly for the moments you want to hand over your phone without handing over your entire photo library too.

What’s in Pro?

Widgets, quick access, themes, and quality settings. The core camera stays simple either way.

Where do the photos go?

Into Lemon Squeezy’s own gallery inside the app. Kids can browse their snaps there, without scrolling through your camera roll.
