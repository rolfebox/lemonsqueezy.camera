Designed for tiny photographers

Lemon Squeezy starts off ultra-simple, then lets you add more controls as your kid grows – so they can learn without getting overwhelmed (or lost).

One huge shutter button little fingers won’t miss

The camera screen is stripped down to the essentials, with a shutter that’s easy to hit. If your kid is still in the “random screen taps” phase, enable Clumsy Fingers Mode to make (almost) the entire screen a tap target.

Photos stay in your app, not the camera roll

Apple’s Camera app makes it far too easy to wander into your whole photo library. Lemon Squeezy keeps kids’ snaps separate in a safe in-app gallery, so they can review their photos without scrolling through yours.

Daily limits and cooldown timers to curb itchy shutter fingers

Set a daily photo limit, and (optionally) a short cooldown after each shot. It’s a simple way to slow things down, reduce rapid-fire duplicates, and keep storage under control.

Nice and simple:

When the daily limit is reached, playtime’s over.

Settings that adapt to your kid as they grow

Start with the basics, then gradually introduce “real camera” skills like tap to focus and pinch to zoom when they’re ready. You decide what’s enabled, and when.

Widgets, quick access, themes, and quality settings*

Pro members can customize the look, add widgets, launch from the Lock Screen, and choose higher (or lower) quality photos depending on your storage needs.

* Available to Pro members.

FAQ

Does this replace the iPhone Camera app?

It can – but it’s mainly for the moments you want to hand over your phone without handing over your entire photo library too.

What’s in Pro?

Widgets, quick access, themes, and quality settings. The core camera stays simple either way.

Where do the photos go?

Into Lemon Squeezy’s own gallery inside the app. Kids can browse their snaps there, without scrolling through your camera roll.
