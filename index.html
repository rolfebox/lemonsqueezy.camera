Lemon Squeezy

An easy peasy camera app for kids

Lemon Squeezy is a minimal camera designed for mini photographers — with a massive shutter button, a kid-safe gallery, and gentle limits that encourage better photos instead of endless rapid-fire duplicates.

 Get Lemon Squeezy

One huge shutter button. No distractions.

The camera is deliberately simple: no modes to get lost in, no confusing controls, and a shutter button that’s easy to hit with tiny hands. Point, tap, done.

Photos stay in the app, not your camera roll.

Kids can browse their own gallery without wandering into your full photo library. Nothing gets shared, exported, or synced unless you choose to do it from the parents area.

Daily limits and shutter delays to encourage focus.

Add a gentle daily photo cap and optional delays between shots to slow things down. Fewer duplicates, more intention — and less storage waste.

Settings that adapt as your kids grow.

Start ultra-simple, then loosen the reins over time. Adjust limits, delays, and other controls to match your kid’s age — without turning the app into a toy box.

No ads. No tracking. No worries.

Lemon Squeezy doesn’t collect personal data, doesn’t track you across apps, and doesn’t monetize attention. It’s a kids camera that’s built for peace of mind.

Ready to try it?

Download Lemon Squeezy on iPhone and hand over the camera without handing over your whole photo library.
