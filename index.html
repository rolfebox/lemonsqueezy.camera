Designed for tiny photographers

Everything here is intentional: fewer buttons, fewer distractions, and fewer ways to accidentally access the rest of your phone.

The giant shutter button

The camera screen is stripped down to the essentials — with a shutter button that expands to take up as much space as possible. Optional “Clumsy Fingers” mode can make (almost) the whole screen a tap target.

Photos stay in the app

Apple’s Camera app is a sneaky back door into your entire library. Lemon Squeezy keeps kids’ snaps in a separate, kid-safe gallery that’s easy to browse and hard to misuse.

Cooldowns and daily limits

Set a daily photo limit and (optionally) a short cooldown after each shot. It’s a simple way to prevent 400 near-identical photos of the ceiling — without you needing to hover.

Nice touch:

When the daily limit is reached, playtime’s over — no bargaining, no digging through settings.

Themes, widgets, and quick launch (Pro)

Customize the look to match your kid’s personality, add a Home Screen widget, and launch from the Lock Screen like the default Camera. Optional upgrades — the core camera stays simple.

Lemon Squeezy is built to be the camera you hand over without immediately regretting it.

FAQ

Does this replace the iPhone Camera app?

It can. But it’s mainly designed for the moments you want to hand your phone to a kid without giving them the keys to the kingdom.

Can kids still zoom and tap to focus?

Yes — those can be available as optional “learning” gestures as they get more confident, while the default experience stays simple.

What’s in Pro?

Extra customization (themes), widgets, quick launch, and quality controls. Keep the core camera free and uncluttered.
