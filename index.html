The giant shutter button

The camera screen is stripped down to the essentials — with a shutter button that’s easy to hit. Optional “Clumsy Fingers” mode can make (almost) the whole screen a tap target.

Photos stay in the app

Apple’s Camera app is a back door into your entire library. Lemon Squeezy keeps kids’ snaps separate in a kid-safe gallery that’s easy to browse.

Cooldowns and daily limits

Set a daily photo limit and an optional cooldown after each shot. It’s a simple way to prevent 400 near-identical photos — without you needing to hover.

Nice touch: When the daily limit is reached, playtime’s over.

Optional Pro upgrades

Themes, widgets, quick launch, and quality controls. The core camera stays simple.

FAQ

Does this replace the iPhone Camera app?

It can, but it’s mainly for the moments you want to hand your phone to a kid without giving them the keys to the kingdom.

Can kids zoom and tap to focus?

Yes — those can be enabled as optional gestures as they get more confident, while the default experience stays simple.

What’s in Pro?

Custom themes, widgets, quick launch, and quality controls. Keep the core camera uncluttered.
