Privacy

Lemon Squeezy is a simple camera app for kids. We do not collect your data.

Effective date: January 22, 2026
Contact: tom@lemonsqueezy.camera

Lemon Squeezy does not track you. We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Lemon Squeezy does not require an account, does not connect to servers, and does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Device permissions

Lemon Squeezy requests the following permissions only to provide core features:

  • Camera – so your child can take photos in the app.
  • Photo library (Add Only) – only if you choose to save a photo to your camera roll.

Where your photos go

Photos remain on your device. We do not upload your photos anywhere. If you choose to save photos to your camera roll, they are saved to the Photos app on your device.
